Gender through rasas

Led by Vivek Vijaykumaran

Previous Next Close

In this project, students will explore the idea of gender across the nine basic emotions (Shringara, Adbuta, Veera, Kroda, Karuna Hasya, Bhaya, Bheebtsa, and Shanta) Students will attempt to understand the role of gender in the way these emotions are triggered and handled. Students will also look at how ‘gender-conditioning’ define the way emotions are experienced. Students will be guided to see the body as the essential starting point. Some of the performance aspects of this project involve working with one’s breath and using various exercises to open up the body and facilitate a free flow of energy. Project participants will collect material to explore these concepts to support or counter these speculations. This will culminate in a performance.
