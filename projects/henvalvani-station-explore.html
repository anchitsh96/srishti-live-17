Zindabad

Artists: Aastha Chauhan and Vipin Bhati

Previous Next Close

In Zindabad, the students interact with the members of Mazdoor Kisan Shakti Sangathan and the School of Democracy in Rajasthan and attempt to understand their efforts in demanding for the Right to Information Act (RTI) and deepening democratic education within the framework of the Indian Constitution. Their interactions and work will be turned into an audio documentary/narrative which will be screened at the Cubbon Park Metro Station.

Aastha Chauhan
