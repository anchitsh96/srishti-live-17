Plastic Bodies

Led by Sujay Saple

Previous Next Close

Plastic Bodies invites the audience to experience a series of very short performative episodes, which have evolved from the students; personal responses and investigations into conversations around gender, identity and violence - as experienced by the body. In turns playful, poignant, participatory and provocative - each short visual escapade places the body at the heart of its creative exploration and formal rendering - inviting audiences to negotiate multiple meanings.
