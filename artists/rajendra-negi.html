Rajendra Negi

Henvalvani Station Explore

Rajendra Negi is a farmer, activist and the founder-director of FM 90.4MHz Henvalvani Community Radio Station located in Chamba, Uttarakhand. He along with a group of young persons started the radio station in the early 2000s. His team has worked on various kinds community-centric programs including awareness campaigns, programs promoting local talents or documenting traditional practices, women empowerment, conservation, environmental history, programs highlighting successful small-scale enterprises, people’s movements in the valley, health capsules, stories of urban migration etc. In partnership with a local NGO of the area, ASTHA, Henvalvani has also organized community melas and youth programs, in an attempt to bring together and broadcast the diverse voices of the hills on one common platform. As a member of the Beej Bachao Andolan, he is also deeply involved with organic farming and its sustainability.
