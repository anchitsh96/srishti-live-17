Thomas Heidtmann

Place In Orbit

Thomas Heidtmann is a media artist formally educated at the Berlin University of the Arts with an emphasis on painting and new media. His work examines forms and expressions of exploration, collaboration, and communication. Thomas is fascinated with space technologies, mirrors, and questions of visibility and observation. He combines traditional aesthetics, materials, and techniques with new technologies such as 3D printing to create installations and objects that are often interactive and engaging. .
