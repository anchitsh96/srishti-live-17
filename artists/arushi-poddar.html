Arushi Poddar

Overcoming(s)

Arushi is a theatre, film and fine arts professional with two and a half decades of experience in the creative and production aspects of acting. She has been involved with amateur and semi-professional theatre in Bangalore as part of Abhinaya, SET and other theatre groups. She has also been deeply involved in the propagation of traditional indian dance, music and craft through participation in seminars and conducting workshops. For over two decades, she has been conducting theatre and creative arts therapy-based workshops for adults, and children at government schools and those with special needs. She is also a practioner of psycho drama, drama therapy, creative arts therapy, theatre of the oppressed and non-violent communication.
