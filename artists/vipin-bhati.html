Vipin Bhati

Project : Zindabad

Vipin Bhati is an independent sound designer and screenwriter. Currently he is also teaching in Srishti as a full time faculty. A graduate of the Film and TV institute of India , he has worked in more than 30 feature films and hundreds of documentaries, commercials and TV programs . He was also a researcher , writer and sound supervisor on Aamir Khan's show "Satyameva Jayate". Vipin continues to work on socially relevant films and projects. Vipin Bhati also authored an audio novel "Sahibabad Stories" that chronicles, in a funny way , the experience of growing up in a subaltern suburb of Delhi in the 90's.
