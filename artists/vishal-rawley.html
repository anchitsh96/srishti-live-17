Vishal Rawley

Eco Pods

Vishal Rawley is a filmmaker, graphic designer and an activist who uses design as a tool for social interaction and community engagement. He has a passion for disseminating information using technology and empowering community through public participation. He makes unconventional objects from boats to robots, tree houses to floating gardens. He has worked on several public installation art projects mostly themed around issues of urban ecology. .
