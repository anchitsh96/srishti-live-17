Vivek Vijayakumaran

Gender through Rasas

Vivek works as a freelance actor and actor trainer. He is also the artistic director of ‘Our Theatre’. As a theatre facilitator and storyteller, he has worked with various schools, NGOs, libraries, bookstores, slums and also corporates. He is also a recipient of the Inlaks India Theatre Award-2012. Vivek was also recently featured in Forbes India life as one of the 12 theatre practitioners who are pushing boundaries and innovating in India today. His most recent work ‘Bhima’, has been in performance since 2015 to much acclaim across the country .
