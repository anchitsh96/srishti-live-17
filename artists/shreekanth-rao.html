Shreekanth Rao

Gender: Outside and Inside the Body

Shreekanth Rao is a theatre artist based in Bangalore. He works on writing, direction and the performance aspects of theatre making. He has written ‘Where the Shadow Ends’ and ‘Witness This’ in English, and has written and directed ‘Naguvaaga Nakku...’ (‘Laughed while we did’) in Kannada. As a performer he has worked on text-based plays and devised performances. He studied Devising Theatre and Performance at the London International School of Performing Arts (LISPA), London. As a performer he has worked with Kriyative Theatre, Indian Ensemble and Bangalore Little Theatre. .
